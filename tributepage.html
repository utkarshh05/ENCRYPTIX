About Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi, born on 17 September 1950, is the 14th and current Prime Minister of India. He has been in office since 26 May 2014. Modi is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), a Hindu nationalist volunteer organization. He is known for his dynamic and decisive leadership, numerous initiatives for economic reform, and efforts to improve India's international relations.

Early Life and Education

Modi was born in Vadnagar, a small town in northeastern Gujarat, into a family of grocers. He helped his father sell tea as a child and later ran his own tea stall. Modi was introduced to the RSS at the age of eight. He has a master's degree in political science from Gujarat University.

Political Career

Modi's political career began in the RSS, and he joined the BJP in 1987. He became the Chief Minister of Gujarat in 2001 and held the position until 2014. Under his leadership, Gujarat saw significant economic growth and development. As Prime Minister, Modi has launched several key initiatives, including Make in India, Digital India, and the Swachh Bharat Mission (Clean India Mission).

During his tenure as the Chief Minister of Gujarat, Modi's government was credited with successfully attracting significant foreign investment and for implementing innovative development projects. As the Prime Minister, his efforts have been directed towards fostering a more inclusive and economically robust India.

Achievements and Initiatives

  • Swachh Bharat Mission: Launched in 2014, this campaign aims to clean up the streets, roads, and infrastructure of India's cities and rural areas.
  • Make in India: An initiative to encourage companies to manufacture their products in India and incentivize dedicated investments into manufacturing.
  • Digital India: A campaign to ensure the government's services are made available to citizens electronically by improving online infrastructure and increasing internet connectivity.
  • Goods and Services Tax (GST): Implemented in 2017, GST is a comprehensive, multi-stage, destination-based tax that is levied on every value addition.
  • Jan Dhan Yojana: A financial inclusion program aimed at expanding affordable access to financial services such as bank accounts, remittances, credit, insurance, and pensions.
  • Ujjwala Yojana: An initiative to provide LPG connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households.
  • Ayushman Bharat: A health scheme to provide insurance cover to economically disadvantaged families.

Personal Life

Narendra Modi is known for his simple lifestyle and strict discipline. He is a vegetarian and often practices yoga. Modi has been noted for his social media presence, where he actively engages with the public.
